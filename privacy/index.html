Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 4, 2026

The Simple Version

📱

Local Storage Only

All your habit data and fasting sessions stay on your device. We never see it.

🚫

No Data Collection

We don't collect, store, or share any personal information. Period.

🛡️

No Tracking

No analytics, no behavioral tracking, no advertising trackers.

Data We Don't Collect

MyDailyTally is designed with privacy as a core principle. Here's what we don't collect:

  • Your habit data or fasting sessions
  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location information
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic information
  • Any form of tracking data

How Your Data Is Stored

All your information is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure data storage mechanisms:

  • Habit Data: Stored in your device's local database, encrypted by iOS
  • Fasting Sessions: Stored locally, never transmitted
  • App Settings: Stored in your device's preferences, stays local
  • No Cloud Sync: Data is not synchronized to iCloud or any other cloud service

When you delete the app, all data is permanently removed from your device. We cannot recover it because we never had access to it.

In-App Purchases

MyDailyTally offers a Premium subscription through Apple's App Store. Here's how this works with your privacy:

  • Apple Handles Everything: All purchase transactions are processed by Apple, not us
  • No Payment Data: We never see your credit card information or payment details
  • Anonymous Verification: We only verify your subscription status through Apple's secure API
  • Apple's Privacy Policy: Purchase data is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy, not ours

Third-Party Services

MyDailyTally does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app operates entirely offline and does not communicate with external servers.

The only external communication is with Apple's App Store for subscription verification, which is handled entirely by iOS and Apple's secure systems.

Data Security

Since all data stays on your device, your privacy is protected by:

  • iOS Security: Apple's built-in device encryption and security measures
  • No Network Transmission: Data never leaves your device, so it can't be intercepted
  • Local Access Only: Only you can access your data through the MyDailyTally app
  • Sandboxed Storage: Data is stored in the app's secure, isolated container

Children's Privacy

MyDailyTally does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since the app operates entirely locally on the device with no data collection, it is safe for users of all ages.

International Users

Because MyDailyTally doesn't collect or transmit any data, there are no international data transfer concerns. Your data stays on your device regardless of your location.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will:

  • Update this page with the new policy
  • Change the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • If the changes are significant, notify users through the App Store update notes

However, our core principle will never change: your data stays on your device.
